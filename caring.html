What is Caring?

* Sharing is caring
some people wrote nice things and put them on cars in the parking lot!
Sometimes kids come and talk about other kids who are having a hard time! They care for others!

* Be kind!
sharing
putting up your hand
watching
listening

* Be compassionate and show you care!
glad for someone else
to ba able to feel for someone else

YouTube Video


* Express gratitude!
How do you express gratitude?
hugs
kindness
give love
look after the needs of them

How do you show your teachers gratitude?
kindness
gifts
"thank you"
handshakes
hugs

Are you grateful for what you have?
Don't forget to be glad for what you have!

We have so much

Forgive others!
This takes up space in your heart and brain that can be used for better things...
Your bitterness is going to eat you up from the inside. It will affect you more than the actual other person does.

Help People in Need.
Local and Global!
There are needs all over. The idea that kids can identify needs and then do something about it!

Take care of YOU!
We need to always spend time recharging ourselves! If WE are good, then we can look after others better.
IF we do NOT look after ourselves, we are less able to deal with conflict, frustration and the needs of others.
